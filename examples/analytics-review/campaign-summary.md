# Example Brevo Analytics Review

This is an example output shape for analytics review. It does not resend or schedule a campaign.

## Performance Summary

- Opens: acceptable relative to prior campaigns.
- Clicks: below target.
- Bounces: needs review if above normal list baseline.
- Unsubscribes: acceptable if within expected range.

## Notable Risks

- Subject may be attracting curiosity without matching the CTA.
- CTA may be too low in the email body.
- Tracking consistency needs review before comparing campaigns.

## Audience And Deliverability Notes

- Confirm the list was the intended audience.
- Review recent list growth source and consent quality.
- Check sender/domain status before the next send.

## Copy And CTA Recommendations

- Move the primary CTA above the fold.
- Make the CTA copy match the promised outcome in the subject.
- Remove secondary links that compete with the primary CTA.

## Segment Or Timing Recommendations

- Split new leads from older inactive leads.
- Test a shorter delay after form submission.
- Exclude recent converters.

## Next Test Plan

1. Draft a revised subject/preheader pair.
2. Move the CTA higher.
3. Add consistent UTM parameters.
4. Send a Brevo test email internally.
5. The user manually schedules the next campaign in Brevo after review.

## Manual Brevo Handoff

Open the campaign or template in Brevo, apply copy and tracking changes, send internal tests, confirm the target segment, then manually send or schedule from Brevo.
