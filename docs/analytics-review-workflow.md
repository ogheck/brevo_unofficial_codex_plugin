# Analytics Review Workflow

Use this workflow when Codex reviews Brevo campaign analytics and recommends improvements.

Codex may inspect analytics and recommend changes. Codex must not send, schedule, launch, or resend campaigns.

## Inputs

Gather:

- Campaign name or ID.
- Audience/list/segment.
- Send date.
- Subject and preheader.
- Opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes, spam complaints, and conversions where available.
- Comparable prior campaigns.
- Offer, CTA, and landing page context.

## Output Sections

Use this structure:

1. Performance Summary.
2. Notable Risks.
3. Audience And Deliverability Notes.
4. Copy And CTA Recommendations.
5. Segment Or Timing Recommendations.
6. Next Test Plan.
7. Manual Brevo Handoff.

## Review Focus

Look for:

- Weak subject/preheader alignment.
- Low click rate despite acceptable opens.
- High bounce or complaint rate.
- Segment mismatch.
- CTA friction.
- Landing page mismatch.
- Missing or inconsistent tracking.

## Manual Brevo Handoff

End with:

- What to change in templates or campaign drafts.
- What segment or audience checks to perform.
- What test to run next.
- Reminder that the user sends or schedules from Brevo.
